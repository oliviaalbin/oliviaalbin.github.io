About Me


Hello!

My name's Olivia Albin and I'm currently a student in Wilfrid Laurier University's User Experience Design (UXD) program. In this program I'm learning a variety of skills in design thinking, graphic design, web design, prototyping, research and more.

Along with the UXD courses I'm taking, I'm also pursuing minors in psychology and community health that will provide me with a broader perspective on the people and systems I design for. With the knowledge and skills I gain I hope to add value to any company I am lucky enough to work with.

This portfolio showcases some of the work I have done in my UXD courses so far including the designs and process I went through to get to the final product, whatever that may be.

Thank you for taking the time to look through some of my work! ^-^
